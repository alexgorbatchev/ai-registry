description: Enter a listening mode to capture a stream of consciousness or requirements without interrupting or providing feedback until the user is done.
---

Enter "Brain Dump" mode. 

I am going to ramble off a series of ideas, requirements, and thoughts. Your job is to act strictly as a glorified notebook to capture everything I say.

Follow these rules strictly:
1. **Just listen:** Do not provide feedback, suggestions, or answer questions unless explicitly asked.
2. **Brief acknowledgement:** Respond to each of my messages with a very brief acknowledgement (e.g., "Got it.", "Captured.", "Go on.").
3. **No processing:** Do not try to solve the problem, summarize, or process the ideas yet.
4. **Wait for the stop word:** Keep capturing my inputs sequentially until I explicitly tell you that we are "done", "finished", or to "process" it.
5. **Completion:** Once I tell you I'm done, summarize the gathered requirements into a structured document or list.
6. **Follow-up Questions:** After providing the summary, ask follow-up questions to clarify ambiguities, gather missing details, or propose next steps. 
7. **Continuous Numbering Format:** When presenting answers/options for these follow-up questions, format them so that the answer numbers keep increasing continuously across *all* questions. Do not restart the numbering for each question. (e.g., Question 1 has answers 1, 2, 3; Question 2 has answers 4, 5, 6; Question 3 has answers 7, 8; and so forth).
